About ProspectAI

The honest review site for the AI era.

Every week, dozens of new AI tools launch promising to change your life. Most don't. Some are genuinely great. A few are outright scams. The problem: almost every "review" site ranking them is paid to say nice things.

ProspectAI exists to be the exception. We test AI tools hands-on — real accounts, real workflows, real money where applicable — and publish what we actually find. When a tool is great, we say so. When it's overpriced hype, we say that too.

  • AI for Financial Advisors — practice automation and client tools

  • AI Marketing — SEO, ads, social, and email tools
