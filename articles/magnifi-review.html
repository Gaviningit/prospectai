Magnifi Review 2025 — The AI Investment Assistant That Searches Like Google

Magnifi lets you search the entire investment universe using plain English. We tested it for 30 days with real money. Here's what we found.

ProspectAI Editorial
June 2025
7 min read
✓ Affiliate disclosure

What if you could search for investments the same way you search Google? That's the core promise of Magnifi — an AI-powered investment platform built by TIFIN, a fintech company backed by J.P. Morgan and Morningstar.

The idea is simple: instead of scrolling through screeners and reading fund fact sheets, you just ask. "Find me ETFs focused on AI with an expense ratio under 0.3%." Magnifi finds them, compares them, and explains the tradeoffs in plain English.

We tested Magnifi for 30 days — free tier and paid — to see whether the AI actually delivers or whether it's another chatbot dressed up as a financial tool.

How Magnifi Works

Magnifi's interface is built around a single search bar. You type what you're looking for — a theme, a goal, a constraint — and the AI surfaces relevant stocks, ETFs, and mutual funds from a universe of over 15,000 securities.

The results aren't just a list. Magnifi scores each option across multiple dimensions, shows you how they compare, and provides an AI-generated explanation of the tradeoffs. It's closer to having a conversation with an analyst than using a traditional screener.

Example search: "Show me healthcare ETFs that have outperformed the S&P 500 over 5 years with low volatility." Magnifi returns ranked results in under 3 seconds with side-by-side comparison and AI commentary.

What We Liked

The natural language search genuinely works. This was the biggest surprise. We expected it to be a novelty — it's not. The AI correctly interpreted nuanced queries like "find dividend stocks with growing payouts in the financial sector" and returned results that matched our criteria accurately.

The comparison tools are excellent. Once you have a shortlist, Magnifi lets you compare options across expense ratio, performance, holdings overlap, risk metrics, and more — all in one view. This would take 30 minutes to do manually.

Portfolio analysis is a hidden gem. Connect your brokerage and Magnifi analyzes your existing holdings for overlap, concentration risk, and sector exposure. It then suggests adjustments using the same natural language interface.

Where It Falls Short

Magnifi is not built for active traders. There's no real-time data, no charting, no signals or alerts. If you're day trading or swing trading, look at Trade Ideas instead.

The free tier is also quite limited — most of the useful features require the paid plan at $14.99/month or $119/year. The free version is essentially a demo.

Pricing

  • Free: Basic search, limited results
  • Premium: $14.99/month or $119/year — full access to all features
  • Magnifi+: Includes managed portfolios (separate pricing)
Our Verdict

Best AI investing tool for research-focused investors.

If you're building or managing a long-term portfolio and want to do proper research without spending hours on screeners, Magnifi is excellent value at $119/year. It won't help you day trade, but for everything else — finding funds, analyzing holdings, comparing options — it's the best AI tool in its category.
