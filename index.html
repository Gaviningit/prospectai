FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to know how to code or use any new software?
Not at all. We build and install the entire system for you. Your only job is to show up to the meetings we book into your calendar.
How long does setup take?
Most advisors are live within 5–7 business days. We handle everything — setup, connecting your tools, testing the full workflow, and handing you a working system.
What happens if a prospect doesn't respond to the first email?
The system automatically sends follow-up messages at strategic intervals — without you lifting a finger. You only get notified when a prospect responds or books a call.
Will the emails sound like me, or will they sound like a bot?
Your voice, not a template. During onboarding we study how you write and calibrate the AI to match your tone. Prospects regularly think the email came directly from you.
Is there a long-term contract?
No contracts. Month-to-month, cancel anytime. The one-time setup fee covers building your custom system — after that it's a flat monthly fee for as long as you use it.
What tools do I need to already have?
Just Gmail and Calendly — tools most advisors already use. We handle everything else. No new software to learn or maintain.
